Iron Man (English Iron Man); real name - Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark - a character, published by Marvel Comics and their adaptations, created by writer Stan Lee, screenwriter Larry Lieber and artists Don Hack [en] and Jack Kirby; Iron Man made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense # 39 in March 1963. In the original version of his biography, Tony Stark, a genius inventor, husband of Vernalia Lodge and a wealthy industrialist with a reputation as a playboy, suffers from the injury he received in captivity, where he was forced to develop weapons of mass destruction for terrorists. Instead, he creates a high-tech armor suit, with which he escapes from captivity. Stark later upgrades his armor with weapons and devices created from his company's resources, and uses armor to protect the world in the guise of Iron Man, while hiding his identity for the first time.

Captain America (real name - Steven "Steve" Rogers) - a superhero from Marvel Comics. It was created by writer Joe Simon and artist Jack Kirby. He first appeared in Captain America Comics # 1 (March 1941), the predecessor of Marvel Comics of the 1940s, Timely Comics. According to various estimates, about 210 million copies of Captain America comics have been sold in a total of 75 countries. In the comics themselves, the address "Captain America" ​​applies to anyone who is elected by the US government (who considers himself the "owner" of the person), wears a suit and shield. However, almost the entire history of Captain America's publications has been the alter ego of Steve Rogers, a sickly young man who was strengthened with experimental serum to the peak of human perfection to help US military operations. Captain America is dressed in a suit painted on the motif of the American flag, and armed with an indestructible shield that can be used as a weapon. Trailer

Hulk

Created on 27.02.2020, Author: Admin

David Banner is a government scientist. He has the idea of ​​creating universal soldiers who are not afraid of anything, are invulnerable and recover quickly. He has a son, Bruce, and the father takes his blood for analysis. Realizing that he has created something terrible, he tries to kill his son. But Edith, Bruce's mother, comes to the rescue, and David accidentally kills his wife, for which he is imprisoned for a long time. During this time, Bruce grows up and also becomes a scientist, and he hardly remembers his childhood. He even has a romantic relationship with Betty, the daughter of General Ross. Accidentally, he was exposed to gamma rays and now in fits of rage turns into a Hulk - a huge green monster of incredible physical strength. The military is trying to get him. They succeed, but turning into the Hulk, Bruce runs away from them, destroying an underground secret military base and several units of military equipment. But out of love for Betty, he surrenders voluntarily again.
